## Problem Statement

Independent pet owners often struggle to manage daily pet care and handle unexpected situations due to busy schedules and lack of immediate support. This leads to missed routines, delayed care, and difficulty in managing emergencies.

---

## Features

*  **Authentication** (Login / Register)
*  **Pet Onboarding**
*  **Home Dashboard**
*  **Reminders**

  * Vaccination
  * Grooming
  * Deworming
  * Medication
*  **SOS Feature**

  * Symptom-based AI analysis
*  **Pet Walk Booking**
*  **Pet Sitter Booking**
